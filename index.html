Quick AI Guides

How to Summarize Text with AI

Copy your long text and ask any AI assistant to "summarize this in 3-4 bullet points." For research papers, specify "academic summary" for better results. This works with ChatGPT, Claude, and most text-based AI tools.

Creating Images with AI

Use detailed descriptions including subject, style, colors, and mood. For example: "A minimalist logo for a coffee shop, using brown and green colors, clean lines." Tools like Midjourney and Canva AI work well for this.

Writing Professional Emails

Provide context like "Write a professional email to my manager requesting time off next week." Specify tone (formal, casual) and key points to include. Most AI writing assistants can generate polished emails quickly.

Using AI for Studying

Upload your study materials and ask AI to create flashcards, practice questions, or simplified explanations. You can also request it to create study schedules based on your available time.

Automating Small Tasks

Identify repetitive tasks like data entry, formatting documents, or organizing information. Describe the task to AI and ask for step-by-step automation suggestions or templates you can use.

AI Tool Comparisons

ChatGPT vs Claude
ChatGPT strengths: Creative writing, coding assistance, general knowledge, plugin ecosystem
Claude strengths: Complex reasoning, ethical considerations, longer context windows, analysis tasks

Best use: Choose ChatGPT for creative projects and coding; Claude for analytical tasks and detailed discussions.

Google Gemini vs Perplexity
Gemini strengths: Integration with Google services, multimodal capabilities, current information
Perplexity strengths: Source citations, research-focused interface, accurate fact-checking

Best use: Gemini for general queries and Google ecosystem tasks; Perplexity for research and verified information.

Canva AI vs Midjourney
Canva AI strengths: User-friendly, integrated design tools, templates, quick social media graphics
Midjourney strengths: High-quality artistic output, detailed control, advanced image generation

Best use: Canva AI for business graphics and quick designs; Midjourney for artistic projects and high-quality visuals.

Useful AI Prompts

For Studying

"Create a study schedule for [subject] with 2 hours daily for 2 weeks, focusing on key concepts."
"Explain [complex topic] in simple terms as if I'm a beginner, with examples."
"Generate 10 practice questions about [topic] with increasing difficulty."

For Productivity

"Break down [project] into manageable tasks with estimated time for each."
"Suggest 5 ways to optimize my workday for better focus and efficiency."
"Create a template for weekly team updates including goals and challenges."

For Writing

"Write a professional email to [recipient] about [topic] with a polite tone."
"Create an outline for a blog post about [topic] with 5 main sections."
"Improve this text to be more engaging and concise: [paste text]"

For Creativity

"Generate 10 creative ideas for [project type] with [specific requirements]."
"Describe a visual concept for [purpose] including colors, style, and mood."
"Brainstorm unique solutions to [problem] considering [constraints]."

Practical AI Tips

Be Specific

Provide clear context and details in your prompts for better results.

Iterate and Refine

Don't settle for the first response. Ask follow-up questions to improve results.

Break Down Tasks

Complex requests work better when divided into smaller, specific prompts.

Verify Important Information

Always fact-check critical information from AI, especially for important decisions.

Use the Right Tool

Different AI tools have different strengths. Match the tool to your specific task.

Provide Examples

When possible, show AI examples of what you want for better understanding.
